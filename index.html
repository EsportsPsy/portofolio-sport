What I do

I design practical, measurable mental performance programs tailored to the sport context (individual athletes and teams), combining applied sport psychology with structured routines and evidence-based tools.

  • Mental PreparationPre-competition routines, goal setting, performance plans, confidence building.
  • Focus & ConcentrationAttention control, cue words, refocus strategies, error recovery.
  • Pressure ManagementStress responses, performance anxiety, clutch moments, decision-making under load.
  • Emotional AwarenessEmotional literacy, regulation strategies, coping plans, resilience training.
  • Team & CommunicationGroup dynamics, feedback culture, cohesion, leadership and role clarity.

Approach

Evidence-based applied sport psychology with practical delivery: individual sessions, staff workshops, on-field/remote interventions, and integrated support for the performance environment.

Methods & Tools
